9 Important Travel Tips

✅ Do

  • Use Grab or trusted taxi services.
  • Keep the guest house address saved on phone.
  • Carry cash plus card backup.
  • Check maps before meetings and restaurant visits.
  • Leave early for airport travel.

⚠️ Don’t

  • Do not exchange money without checking the rate.
  • Do not depend only on cash.
  • Do not leave passport carelessly.
  • Do not board unknown rides without route checking.
  • Do not wait until the last minute for airport departure.
